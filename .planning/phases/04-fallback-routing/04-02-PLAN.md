**Critical placement rules** (04-RESEARCH.md § 1):
- `##` block IMMEDIATELY precedes the documented element. NO blank lines, NO `#` (single-hash) interruptions inside the `##` block.
- Annotation goes BETWEEN the `##` block and the member, NEVER between the `##` block and the annotation. Pattern: `## block\n@export var foo`.
- Structural tags use `@keyword:` form with NO space before colon. `@tutorial: URL`, `@experimental`, `@deprecated` — NEVER `@tutorial : URL`.
- BBCode tags must be CLOSED (`[code]...[/code]`, `[b]...[/b]`); only `[br]` self-closes.

**Tag-set summary** (D-04-03):
- Structural: `@tutorial(label)`, `@experimental`, `@deprecated`. Phase 4 uses ZERO `@deprecated` per RESEARCH § 8 Pitfall #3.
- BBCode: `[param x]`, `[code]...[/code]`, `[Class TileMapLayer]`, `[method foo]`, `[member bar]`, `[constant Color.RED]`, `[signal Class.changed]`.
